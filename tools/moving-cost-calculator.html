Average Moving Costs in 2026

Local moves typically cost $800–$2,500 for a 2-bedroom home. Long-distance moves average $2,500–$6,000 for a 2-bedroom. Cross-country moves can range from $4,000 to $10,000 or more depending on volume and distance.

Full-service moves (pack and move) cost 30–50% more than labor-only services where you do the packing.

Pro tip: Moving in the middle of the week or month is typically 15–25% cheaper than weekends and month-end, when demand is highest.
