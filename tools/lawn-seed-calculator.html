Grass Seed Coverage Rates

Seeding rates vary by grass type. Cool-season grasses like fescue and bluegrass typically require 2–4 lbs per 1,000 sq ft for new seeding. Overseeding an existing lawn requires about half that rate.

Pro tip: Seed in fall for cool-season grasses, and in late spring for warm-season grasses. Soil temperature matters more than air temperature for germination.
