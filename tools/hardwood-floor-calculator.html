Hardwood Flooring Waste Guide

Straight installation: 10% waste. Diagonal: 15%. Herringbone or parquet patterns: 20%. Always buy slightly more than calculated — leftover boards are valuable for future repairs.

Pro tip: Let hardwood planks acclimate in the room for 3–5 days before installation to prevent warping after install.
